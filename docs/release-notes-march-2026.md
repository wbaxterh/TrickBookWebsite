# Release Notes — March 2026

**The Trick Book v3.0 — The Social Update 🏂🛹**

We've been busy building! Here's everything new in The Trick Book this month.

---

## 🤖 Meet Kaori — Your AI Snowboard Companion

Say hello to **Kaori ❄️**, The Trick Book's first AI companion! Inspired by Kaori Nishidake from SSX Tricky, she's a cheerful snowboard-obsessed AI who's always down to chat about tricks, gear, spots, and everything shred-related.

- **She's already your homie** — find her in your Messages or Homies tab
- **Always online** — message her anytime, she responds in real-time
- **Trick knowledge** — ask her about snowboard tricks, she'll share tips and hype you up
- **Full rider profile** — check out her About page for the SSX Tricky backstory

*More AI companions are coming soon — a skateboarder, surfer, BMXer, and more!*

## 💬 Direct Messaging — Fully Revamped

Send messages to your homies with a completely redesigned chat experience:

- **Real-time messaging** — messages appear instantly via WebSocket
- **Typing indicators** — see the animated dots when someone is typing
- **Read receipts** — double-check marks when your message is read
- **Shared content** — share TrickLists, tricks, spots, and videos directly in chat
- **Unread badges** — never miss a message

---

## 📍 3,800+ Skate Spots Worldwide

We've massively expanded the spots database:

- **3,805 spots** across the US, Canada, Australia, Denmark, Norway, and more
- **Famous spots included** — El Toro, Hubba Hideout, Hollywood High, Carlsbad Gap
- **Spot reviews** — rate and review spots you've visited
- **Search & filter** — find spots by state, type, or name

## 🏆 Spot Trick History

Ever wonder what tricks have been landed at a famous spot? Now you can see:

- **Trick history on spot pages** — see what's been landed and by who
- **Submit your own** — log tricks you've landed at any spot
- **Upvote system** — give props to the sickest sends
- **Pre-loaded history** — famous spots come with legendary trick data

## 🔗 Tricks ↔ Spots ↔ Feed — Everything Connected

Your trick data is now linked across the entire app:

- **Tag spots on your TrickList** — link tricks to where you landed them
- **Add video proof** — attach video URLs to specific tricks
- **Spot tags on feed uploads** — tag a spot when you post a video
- **Spot badges & video badges** — visual indicators on your TrickList items
