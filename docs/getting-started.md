# Getting Started with TrickBook

**Your journey to tracking tricks starts here.**

Welcome to TrickBook! This guide will help you get started with the app and make the most of its features.

## Creating Your Account

1. Download TrickBook from the App Store
2. Open the app and tap "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. Choose your favorite action sport categories

## Your First Trick List

Once you're signed in, you can create your first trick list:

1. Go to the Trickbook tab
2. Tap "Create New List"
3. Name your list (e.g., "2026 Goals")
4. Start adding tricks from Trickipedia

## Tracking Progress

Mark tricks as:
- **Learning** - You're working on it
- **Landed** - You've done it at least once
- **Consistent** - You can land it regularly
